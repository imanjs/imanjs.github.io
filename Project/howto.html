Matchin' Markup


How To Play

Matchin' Markup is easy to play! Players are presented with multiple cards, each featuring a subject on the front and content on the back. Some cards will have the subject on the front, like "Tags," and a related word on the back, such as "Link." Other cards will display the subject on the front and the correct answer on the back. For instance, the front might show "Tags" and the back will reveal "<a>" as the answer.

Steps To Play Matchin' Markup

  1. Understand: Cards have subjects on the front and content on the back.
  2. Start Matching: Begin by flipping over one card to reveal the subject.
  3. Find the card: Sort the remaining and see the corresponding content.
  4. Flip the card: Found the match? Turn over to verify if you're correct.
  5. Continue matching: Repeat steps 3-5 for all cards until completed.
  6. Check your score: Find your score in the top right corner at the end!
